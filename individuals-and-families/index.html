individuals and families

At Ameritas, we are in the business of fulfilling life. And for individuals and families, this means reducing uncertainty, helping grow assets and protecting what is most cherished.

Life insurance can provide your family members the resources to maintain their lifestyle when you die. It can replace some or all of your income, pay off debts, cover funeral costs and can even help fund longer-range needs like college tuition or retirement. Insure your spouse or partner as well, even if he or she doesn’t work outside the home.

Disability insurance is also important. It can replace a portion of your income if you are unable to work due to a disabling illness or injury.

Dental and vision health are also very important. Regular exams and cleanings from your dentist help teeth and gums stay healthy. And we keep hearing about the growing body of evidence linking our oral health with our general health.

It’s never too soon to think about retirement and investing in the future. At Ameritas, we have plans and products that can help you protect and invest for you and your loved ones.
