fiduciary assistance

Helping you manage important responsibilities

The Employee Retirement Income Security Act (ERISA) protects the rights of participants and beneficiaries of retirement plans. We are prepared to help you understand your duties as a fiduciary by providing a wide range of support.

Fiduciary Handbook

We provide a Fiduciary Handbook plan sponsors can use to help manage fiduciary responsibilities. It contains:

  • An overview of fiduciary responsibilities
  • Information on ERISA Section 404(c)
  • Sample investment policy statement
  • A simplified plan review checklist

Fiduciary Services

To help manage the risks and responsibilities of operating a retirement program, we have teamed up with Mesirow Financial Investment Management, Inc. (Mesirow Financial) to provide two levels of fiduciary protection for the selection and monitoring of investment options for a retirement plan.

  • Mesirow Financial 3(21) service offers a "help me do it" flexible approach to providing fiduciary coverage for investment selection and monitoring.
  • Mesirow Financial 3(38) service offers a "do it for me" approach for investment selection and monitoring. Mesirow Financial assumes full discretion for selecting, monitoring, and replacing the investment options, if necessary.
