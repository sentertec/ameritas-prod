We'd love to have you join our team. But first, you'll need to be licensed and appointed with us to sell our products. Some states will require producers to be pre-appointed before presenting a quote to a client.
To join our team:

  • Become appointed with Ameritas of New York and compliant with HIPAA Privacy regulations. Just complete the Appointment Application / Business Associate Addendum.
  • Mail or fax the application and addendum, along with a copy of your license, to the Group Sales Office nearest you.
  • You have the option of depositing your commissions directly to your account. Just complete and include the Direct Deposit Authorization form.
