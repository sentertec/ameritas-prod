My Dental Plan® and 20/20 Plus® Vision Plans let you purchase the level of coverage that's right for your needs and budget. Check out each plan for benefit information and rates.

My Dental Plan® Highlights

  • All plans pay a benefit toward covered services after the $50 per-person deductible.
  • We offer plans with either one or two exams and cleanings per year.
  • The Dental Rewards® feature allows qualifying individuals to carryover an additional amount to add to their following year's annual maximum.
  • Plan 3 includes a $100 vision benefit to use for vision exams, frames, lenses and contact lenses.
  • Save on prescription medications through Walmart or Sam's Club pharmacies. To find a pharmacy near you, visit Walmart.com.

20/20 Plus Vision Plan Highlights

  • All plans offer vision, dental and hearing care benefits with no waiting or elimination periods.
  • Plan C offers increasing annual maximum benefits.
  • Enjoy the freedom to visit the vision care provider of your choice.
  • Save up to 15% off eyewear frames and lenses purchased at any Walmart Vision Center nationwide. To find a Walmart vision center near you, visit Walmart.com.
  • Receive savings on prescription medications at Walmart or Sam's Club pharmacies, and eyewear frames and lenses purchased at any Walmart Vision Center. To find a Walmart pharmacy or vision center near you, visit Walmart.com.
