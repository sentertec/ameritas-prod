Wouldn’t it be great if members were rewarded for practicing good oral wellness? With Dental Reward’s increasing annual maximum feature, they are. Dual Choice Option

Hearing

SoundCare®

Thanks to the cranked-up volume of modern life, hearing loss is becoming a major health concern. It's striking at younger ages than ever before, and the problem is expected to grow. Our SoundCare coverage options for hearing exams, hearing aids and hearing aid maintenance are sound additions to a competitive benefits package.

Individual

My Dental Plan® and 20/20 Plus®

Our individual plans help individuals and their families keep their smiles and vision healthy. Since coverage is not tied to a group plan (such as through an employer), people can count on their protection regardless of employment status, retirement or age. For added value, members also receive savings on medical prescriptions at Walmart and Sam's Club pharmacies nationwide.

Those who can benefit from our individual plans include people who have no employer-sponsored coverage, such as seasonal and part-time workers or those entering retirement; membership organizations ineligible for group coverage; and people who are self-employed. The My Dental Plan and 20/20 Plus include three plan options so individuals can select what works best for their dental and vision health needs. Please Note: 20/20 Plus Plans are not available in New York. Plans are insured by Ameritas. Individual plans are administered by HealthPlan Services.

Value-added Discounts

Prescription Drug Savings

Just for participating in our group dental, vision or hearing care plans, members and their covered dependents (even their pets) can save big on prescription medications through one of the world’s largest retailers. No additional cost. Only savings. Save at any Walmart or Sam’s Club pharmacy nationwide. This Rx discount is offered at no additional cost, and it is not insurance. Even if employees already have health insurance pharmacy benefits, they are welcome to check out this Rx discount.

Eyewear Savings

When members participate in our group dental, Vision Perfect, FUSION, LASIK Advantage or SoundCare plans, they can save on eyewear frames and lenses purchased at any Walmart Vision Center nationwide. It’s available without any additional cost to the plan premium.

Please note, this extra savings is not available on Walmart contact lenses purchases. And because our Focus and ViewPointe plans already include network savings, these plans are excluded from this discount.
