My Dental Plan® lets you purchase the level of coverage that's right for your needs and budget. Check out each plan for benefit information and rates.

My Dental Plan Highlights

  • All plans pay a benefit toward covered services after the $50 per-person deductible.
  • We offer plans with either one or two exams and cleanings per year.
  • The Dental Rewards® feature allows qualifying individuals to carryover an additional amount to add to their following year's annual maximum.
  • Plan 3 includes a $100 vision benefit to use toward vision exams, frames, lenses and contact lenses.
  • Save on prescription medications through Walmart or Sam's Club pharmacies. To find a Walmart pharmacy near you, visit Walmart.com.
