commercial mortgage - loan programs

Life is unpredictable. Even with the best of planning, you can find yourself in need of money for a host of short-term needs – home improvements, new car, higher than expected tuitions costs. If you are an investor with Ameritas Investment Corp, you can access the money you need from your investment portfolio, using your assets in your brokerage and managed accounts as collateral. And, you “borrow” without reducing your investment; your assets keep working for you; and your investment strategy remains undisturbed. Because you are not selling any assets to get the cash you need, there are also no capital gains to worry about.

Nothing is without risk, of course, if the market value of your portfolio declines, you may need to deposit additional funds or securities into your account, or, liquidate some securities to satisfy the margin requirements. Check with your AIC Advisor to learn more about lending options and any tax implications that might affect you.
