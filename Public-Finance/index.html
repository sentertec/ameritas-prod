Ameritas Investment Corp. (AIC) through its Public Finance Group is a leading investment banking firm in the state of Nebraska.  Our mission is to provide financing to fund public improvements.  AIC’s very experienced and knowledgeable team of investment bankers works with community leaders to raise the necessary capital through bond issues geared toward enhancing the quality of life in Nebraska communities.  Clients include:

Municipalities

Public Finance has worked with cities of all sizes in a wide-range of community development projects by managing and marketing their bond issues.

Health Care

An important source of funding for health care facilities is bonds. Our Public Finance team has underwritten bonds for all types and sizes of health care facilities.

School Districts

Public Finance provides leadership to school districts for special elections and other bond issues, in addition to underwriting and issuing those bonds.

Public Power and Utilities

Public Finance is dedicated to its public power and utilities clients, always prepared to manage any regulatory complexities that often accompany these bond issues.

Sanitary Improvement Districts, Natural Resources Districts and Water Districts

Our team of Public Finance professionals is experienced in all of these areas and can help you evaluate and execute your financing options.

Higher Education

Financing of public and private higher education is unique and requires the comprehensive scope of experience that our Public Finance group brings to the process.

Special projects

The previous list of clients is not all-inclusive.  Public Finance has been involved with specialty bond issues of all types, including Industrial Development Revenue Bonds and other nongovernmental financing.  We welcome clients with special projects.
