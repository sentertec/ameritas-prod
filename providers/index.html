Welcome, all dental providers! You’ve come to the right place.

As a nationally recognized dental insurance carrier, Ameritas and Ameritas of New York insure or administer dental benefits for more than 5.4 million people across the country. That’s a lot of smiles, and helping keep those smiles healthy is at the heart of what we do.

Sure we have one of the nation’s largest Participating Provider Organization (PPO) dental networks, and the service, claims processing and technological expertise to back it up. But we also work with many dental providers who are not part of our network. It all matters because, as you well know, maintaining good dental health translates to overall wellness, and that’s what the dental industry is all about.

We value our dental providers. We also constantly recruit providers to join our PPO dental network to meet the needs of our dental plan policyholders. When you participate in our network, you are part of a growing company that can increase your chair traffic — and your bottom line.

You can believe it when we say that we take as much pride in the quality of our dental providers as you do in maintaining a quality practice.

For us, it’s about more than just building our dental network. It’s about building and maintaining strong relationships and fulfilling lives.
