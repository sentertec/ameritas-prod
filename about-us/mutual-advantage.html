mutual advantage

We believe being part of a mutual organization is the best way to develop long-term strategies and make decisions benefitting our policyholders, who are our members. This perspective and customer focus guides our actions every day.

Our mutual advantage includes our commitment to do what’s best for the diverse needs of individuals, families and businesses rather than third-party stockholders; to focus on long-term financial strength rather than short-term quarterly returns; and re-investing our proceeds into growing our capital helps ensure we’ll be there when needed most. This mutual advantage has been the foundation that has allowed us to deliver on our promises for more than 125 years.
