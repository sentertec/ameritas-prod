businesses and groups

At Ameritas, we’re in the business of fulfilling life., and as a small-business owner, you’re in business to provide for yourself, your family, your employees and your customers. No matter what your business, a well-conceived insurance and benefits program is essential. From life insurance and investments, to group retirement, dental and vision plans, Ameritas can help you protect what you’ve worked so hard to build.

Our products and services can help you solve many of these types of questions:

What will happen to my business and family if I die or become disabled?

What will happen if certain key employees die or become permanently disabled?

How can I attract and retain the best employees?

How can I help ensure that my business will be able to weather unforeseen financial hardships?

What will happen to my business when I retire?
