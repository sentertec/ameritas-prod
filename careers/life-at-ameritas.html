It’s what we do

Who we are is defined by those that live our mission – making the lives of our customers and their families better – it’s what we do and we strive to do it better than anyone.

The Hours Project from Ameritas on Vimeo.

Our associates are involved in what is important to them – fund raising, community events, marathons, concerts in the park – whatever it is that fulfills their life, we want you to be a part of that. At Ameritas we celebrate each other and what we do. We’re proud of who we are and who we support.

We are where are people are. Ameritas has several locations, which allows greater flexibility for growth and learning opportunities. Not only that, we’re proud to support the communities that we live in.

If you’re thinking about being a part of our team, we want you to know that there is plenty to do – not just for you, but, for your family in each of our locations. We have a lot to be proud of. You’ll see a lot of those things on the chamber websites above, but, nationally, our communities are known for several things.
