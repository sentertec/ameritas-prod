your health & well-being

On a daily basis, we make choices for those we hold closest

Those choices can sometimes involve risk and uncertainty. Choosing the road or means by which we maintain and ensure the health of ourselves and our families is an important decision. Ameritas strives to provide multiple options to fit the needs of our diverse population of associates.

It's simple - we help keep you healthy... you help keep us strong.

Health and Welfare Benefits

Ameritas gives you choices. We know that how each associate manages the well-being of their family is different. We provide detailed resources for associates to understand each of the different plans and options available so they can choose what works best for them.

Medical

From traditional deductible to high deductible plans, we offer multiple plans to choose from, you are sure to find that meets your needs.

Dental and Vision

Comprehensive courage provided by Ameritas Dental and Vision Service Plan (VSP) including Lasik and Hearing benefits.

Healthy Pregnancy Programs

Enrolled associates receive educational information and the support you need to make the best choices for you and your baby. Special incentives are offered for adhering to the programs’ guidelines through the end of the pregnancy!

Flexible Spending Accounts

Health Care and Dependent Care flexible spending accounts allow you to use tax-free dollars to pay for eligible expenses; plus for your convenience there is a debit card you can use.

Domestic Partner Benefits

Non-traditional family units are recognized under the health plan and provided medical benefits to meet their needs.

Tobacco-Free Workplace

Ameritas is committed to providing a health and safe environment for our associates, customers, and visitors as well as to promoting the health and well-being of our associates. To contribute to this, Ameritasis a Tobacco-Free Workplace. We believe that being tobacco-free is the right thing to do to help ensure the health and wellness of our most valuable asset – our people.

Time Away From Work

Time away from work is necessary for many reasons – to rest and recharge on vacation, or to recuperate when you’re sick or to take care of personal and family responsibilities. Ameritas understands that your need for time away is as individual as you are. We offer many types of time away from work to help reduce your worries.

Paid Time Off (PTO) Holidays

Work-life balance is crucial for professional success and endurance. We know that your job satisfaction hinges on a good balance. Our Ameritas policies encourage you to get away and enjoy life.

Leaves of Absence

A variety of leaves (military, civil, adoption, parental/elder leave, etc.) ensure that you have the flexibility to pursue those life events that are meaningful to you.

New Parent Leave

Ameritas recognizes the benefit of supporting new mothers and fathers following the birth of a child/children with time off to care for, bond or arrange for the welfare of the child/children or to handle other family matters. Additional leave may be available under other leave provisions.

Volunteer Leave

We understand that you have things that are important to you outside of work. We also believe it is our responsibility as a good corporate citizen to help enrich our surrounding communities. We encourage our associates to become involved in their communities, lending their voluntary support and leadership to programs that positively impact the quality of life within these communities.

Work-Life Benefits

Balancing a career with a full life outside of work can be a challenge. The Work-Life component of your Total Rewards includes a variety of programs that can help you achieve success at both work and home.

Employee Assistant program (EAP)

Life happens. This confidential service provides professional counseling services to help you and your family better manage your health and your life.

Wellness Programs

Annual health risk assessments, health fairs, flu vaccinations, blood pressure management, Weight Watcher’s at work programs, chair massages and ergonomic work station setup are offered to enhance your personal well-being. Discounts and incentive bonuses are offered throughout many of these programs to keep associates on track & successful!

Fitness

Fitness reimbursement program, on site fitness centers (available in many locations), Unified Strides walking program, and discounts at local health and fitness facilities provides you the support and flexibility for you to stay active.

Matching Charitable Contributions

When something is important to you, it becomes important to us. This program allows your contributions to have twice the impact on charitable organizations. Charitable organizations made by associates to organizations recognized as being tax-exempt under IRS code 501 (c)3 will be matched by the company up to $500 per associate per calendar year.

Alternative Work Schedules

To accommodate and reflect individuals needs and preferences, we offer telecommuting, part-time, flextime, compressed work weeks and varied starting and ending work hours, as business needs allow.
